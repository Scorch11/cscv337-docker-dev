About Us

For over three generations, Granny's Pies has been serving up slices of heaven to the local community and visitors alike. Our story begins in the quaint kitchen of our beloved Granny, whose passion for pie-making turned into a family legacy. With secret recipes passed down through the years, each pie is a taste of tradition and love.

Our journey from a humble home-based business to a beloved local pie shop is a testament to the dedication and hard work of our family and the irresistible appeal of Granny's recipes. We take pride in using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to ensure every pie is perfect. From the flakiest crusts to the richest fillings, Granny's Pies offers a wide range of flavors that cater to every palate.

Whether you're craving a classic apple pie or looking to indulge in something more adventurous like our seasonal specialties, we welcome you to visit us and discover why Granny's Pies are the talk of the town!
